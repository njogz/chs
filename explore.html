what Choice offers

Economy Rooms

Spacious,bright and outward facing rooms,the rooms come with a luxurious double bed,a modern fully equipped spacious bathroom finished with top quality coloured ceramic and an independent entrance.The rooms are also fitted with flat screen TVs,ironing facilities,blow driers and complimentary wi-fi internet.All the rooms also have spacious balconies providing great sunset views.

Premium Rooms

This are superior guest rooms that compliment the services and features of the other rooms.the rooms are equipped with double bed or two individual beds,a complete spacious walk in bathroom with modern design,lined with top quality ceramics.In addition,we have fitted flat screen TVs,ironing facilities, satellite channels and complimentary wi-fi internet.

Supreme Rooms

This are superior category rooms that compliment services and facilities of the premium rooms.the rooms are spacious,bright,outward facing rooms.the rooms come with double bed or twin beds,a modern,fully equipped bathroom,a lounge, kitchen, ironing facilities,blow driers,mini bar,flat screen TV, satellite channel,complimentary wi-fi and large balconies overlooking lake Victoria.

About Us

Choice hotel suites,located in mbita,homabay county is a privately owned hotel near the shores of lake Victoria.it offers a unique opportunity to experience the lakeside views as well as wildlife experiences,such as bird watching at the birds island and game drives at Runs national park promises undiscovered wildlife treasures and undisturbed peace,also the last remaining sanctuary for the roan antelope in Kenya.

The hotel offers 19 exquisite rooms.
